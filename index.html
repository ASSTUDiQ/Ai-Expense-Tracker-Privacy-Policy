Welcome to Ai Expense Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Ai Expense Tracker does not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, our app uses third-party services that may collect certain data:

3. Local Data & Privacy (Financial & Biometric)

  • Financial Data: All your transactions, income, and expense details are stored locally on your device using a secure database. This data is never transmitted to any external server or shared with us.
  • Biometric Data: If you enable Fingerprint or Face Lock, the authentication is handled entirely by the Android System. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your biometric templates.

4. How We Use Information

  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

5. Data Storage & Security

Ai Expense Tracker stores all data locally. You have full control over your data. You can export your data as PDF/CSV or clear it anytime by clearing the app cache/data from device settings.
